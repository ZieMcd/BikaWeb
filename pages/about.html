About Bika

Bika LIMS came into existence in 2002 and Bika Lab Systems Pty Ltd incorporated in 2005. In the Western Cape, South Africa. Founders Lemoene Smit and Inus Scheepers envisaged an agile independent operation, using modern robust Open Source tools and the Internet to ensure quality, and contain cost

Pure Open Source is not a money spinning activity, but offered a holistic solution to systems development and an opportunity to work with inspirational people in a spirit of liberty

Helped along by generous clients and the energy of subsequent shareholders Anneline Sweetnam an Campbell McKellar-Basset, Bika LIMS advanced to a feature rich and stable version 3 in 2015, with branches for health care, environmental management, cannabis and interlaboratory proficiency testing

The project pulled international participation, and other contributors were disadvantaged by both Bika project and company using the same moniker. Bika 4 designs focussing on performance and maintainability, manifested as Senaite LIMS early 2019, with most new code now generated in the Northern Hemisphere

Bika Lab Systems continues to contribute to Senaite but maintains the Bika brand as a skin of add-on customisations and configurations per lab discipline from Cape Town, South Africa, serving a global client base
