Bika Demonstration installations


Over time, distinct Bika branches per lab discipline
developed:
 Bika Health for health care laboratories
 Bika Water for environmental management
 Bika Cannabis for regulated industries
 Bika Interlab for interlaboratory proficiency testing
Standard Bika LIMS for chemistry and microbiology. Most labs
only interface their instruments and configure the rest for a
good fit

Bika Health adds health care functionality: Patients, Doctors
and Caregivers, Referring Institutes and Clinical Cases. It is also
suitable for veterinary settings - Animals as Patients, Vets as
Doctors. It is typically interfaced with EMR or hospital systems

The Bika Covid is a configuration for batch processing and viral
screening

Bika Water is our environmental management LIMS

Bika Cannabis is customised for regulated industries
